About Mkulima Poultry

Mkulima Poultry is a leading provider of high-quality poultry products, committed to delivering fresh and healthy chicken to our customers. With a focus on sustainability and animal welfare, we ensure that our poultry is raised in the best conditions possible.


Founded with a mission to provide premium poultry products, we pride ourselves on our strict quality control measures and dedication to customer satisfaction. Our range of products includes whole chickens, chicken parts, and specialty items, all produced with the highest standards of hygiene and care.


At Mkulima Poultry, we believe in transparency and building trust with our customers. We invite you to experience the difference of premium poultry and join our growing community of satisfied customers.
